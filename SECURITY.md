## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of Notiq seriously. If you have discovered a security vulnerability, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

Please do not open an issue on the tracker for security vulnerabilities.

Instead, please report vulnerabilities by emailing <chinonsoneft@gmail.com>. We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and provide an estimated timeline for resolution.
